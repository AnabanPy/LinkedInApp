# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [ДАТА]

## Введение

LinkJob ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Registration Data**: First name, last name, middle name, email address, phone number, username, password
- **Profile Information**: Profile photo, job postings, messages
- **Job Applications**: Information you submit when applying for jobs

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information and identifiers
- Usage data and analytics
- Log files

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Notify you about changes to our service
- Allow you to participate in interactive features
- Provide customer support
- Gather analysis or valuable information to improve our service
- Monitor the usage of our service
- Detect, prevent and address technical issues
- Send notifications about new messages

## Data Storage

Your data is stored using:
- **Local Storage**: Room database on your device
- **Cloud Storage**: Firebase Firestore for synchronization
- **Authentication**: Firebase Authentication for secure login

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase** (Google): For authentication, database, and storage
- Analytics services may collect anonymous usage data

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Где разместить:

1. **GitHub Pages** (бесплатно):
   - Создайте репозиторий
   - Создайте файл `index.html` или `privacy-policy.html`
   - Включите GitHub Pages в настройках
   - Получите ссылку вида: `https://вашusername.github.io/privacy-policy`

2. **Firebase Hosting** (бесплатно):
   ```bash
   firebase init hosting
   # Создайте HTML файл
   firebase deploy
   ```

3. **Google Sites** (бесплатно):
   - Создайте сайт на sites.google.com
   - Скопируйте содержимое
   - Получите публичную ссылку
